The Oceans Are Trying to Help, But They Need Our Help Too

What’s happening?

Oceans absorb a large amount of CO₂, helping to cool the planet. However, as the oceans warm and become more acidic due to pollution, their ability to absorb CO₂ decreases, which can accelerate climate change.

How can we solve that as a group?

Governments and industries must work together to reduce ocean pollution and prevent further warming. Supporting global policies that protect marine ecosystems and reducing plastic waste can help preserve ocean health.

How can I solve that as an individual?

You can help by reducing plastic use, conserving water, and supporting sustainable fishing practices. Additionally, voting for leaders who advocate for ocean protection and pollution reduction can drive change on a larger scale.
